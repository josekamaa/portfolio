Daylight Collections Boutique Management System

The Daylight Collections Boutique Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to help boutique owners manage their inventory, sales, and customer data efficiently. The system includes features such as:

  • Inventory Management: Track stock levels and product details.
  • Sales Tracking: Record and analyze sales data.
  • Customer Management: Store and manage customer information.
  • Reporting: Generate reports for sales, inventory, and customer insights.

This project was developed using PHP for the backend, MySQL for the database, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript for the frontend. It was a great opportunity to work closely with a client to deliver a tailored solution that meets their specific needs.
